Hope for coronavirus with hope for Christ

Our current situation with coronavirus reminds us:

  • We are mortal beings that can die at any time
  • Our resources such as food, supplies and even money can be depleted during these uncertain times
  • Anxiety, worry and fear can creep into out minds and our loved ones during a crisis

Where does anyone find hope and peace during this pandemic?

Remember:

John 3:16

For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life

Jeremiah 29:11

For I know the plans I have for you declares the Lord, plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future

Psalm 94:19

When anxiety was great within me, your consolation brought joy to my soul

Remember ... to put your faith in the Lord Jesus Christ
